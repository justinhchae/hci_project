Court Observation Dashboard

Integrity

Judges are expected to have integrity, meaning they treat everyone in a fair and ethical manner.

Temperament

Judges are expected to have a judicial temperament, meaning they show compassion and respect toward everyone in the courtroom.

Communication

Judges are expected to communicate their ideas clearly, meaning they can be understood by all parties involved.

Administrative Performance

Judges are expected to be the administrator of their courtroom, meaning they are prepared for court proceedings and maintain control of the courtroom throughout those proceedings.
